Kendra Blackett-Dibinga

Founder of Bikram Yoga Works, she is an innovator in the wellness space by integrating technology to expand access to yoga and wellness services, particularly in African American communities. Kendra is also the co-founder of the fitness program “BEE CAVE Boxing." She has a Bachelor's degree in Education. In addition, she has a lot of experience in dance, particularly African and Caribbean dance styles. She constantly prompts health and wellness within ignored communities which is why she created the non-profit organization “BEE CAVE Boxing." Kendra is also a dedicated mother and advocate for youth voices/empowerment.
